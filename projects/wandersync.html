WanderSync Travel Management App
WanderSync Blog Page LinkedIn Post

Throughout fall 2024, I, along with 5 other developers, worked to build WanderSync: a collaborative travel management system. Together, we developed an Android app while closely following development strategies like AGILE and performing regular code reviews and scrum meetings to ensure our goals stayed aligned. We used scalable design patterns such as singletons, observer models, and low coupling to ensure that different app components could be easily reused and integrated. I was primarily in charge of designing our backend databse with Google Firebase and ensuring that user data could be easily accessed and verified. Additionally, I outlined initial development during each Sprint, as ensuring a reliable database was crucial to the rest of the team's development.
